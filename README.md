# Belle Digital Studio

Official website for **Belle Digital Studio**, a boutique digital studio focused on brand identity, web design, and digital product development.

The site showcases selected client work, case studies, and the studio’s approach to designing clear, conversion-focused digital experiences for modern businesses.

Live site:  
https://www.belledigitalstudio.com/

---

## About the Studio

Belle Digital Studio works with boutique businesses and professional services to create thoughtful digital experiences that combine strong branding with modern web technology.

Services include:

- Brand Identity
- Website Design
- Web Development
- Digital Strategy
- Content & Visual Design

The goal of the studio is to build websites and digital products that are not only visually compelling, but also structured to convert visitors into clients.

---

## Project Purpose

This project represents the studio’s public website and portfolio.

It highlights:

- selected client projects
- design and branding work
- the studio’s website development process
- case studies demonstrating real business outcomes

The site is designed to reflect a minimal, premium design language while maintaining fast performance and clean front-end architecture.

## Design Philosophy

The studio follows a simple principle:

**Websites should not only look good — they should communicate clearly and convert effectively.**

Every project begins with structure and positioning before moving into design and development. This ensures that the final product is aligned with both brand identity and business goals.
