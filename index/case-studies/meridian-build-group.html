The Problem

18 years of expertise. Zero presence.

Meridian had an exceptional track record — $240M+ in delivered projects, fully licensed, deeply experienced. Their website looked like it was built in 2009. Generic template, emoji service icons, no credentials visible.

The gap between who they were and how they presented was costing them developer and architect referrals. The kind of clients who research before they call.

The Approach

Dark. Structural. Built to command respect.

We chose a dark charcoal palette with raw bronze-gold accents — industrial precision, not lifestyle warmth. Bold all-caps typography signals structural confidence. Credentials are the hero element, not decorative photography.

The identity was built to be instantly trusted by architects, developers, and project managers who make decisions based on track record and proven performance.
